Working in the US

There are many ways, but the most feasible visas are:
- H1B visa: A work visa that allows you to start working from day one. It should be applied through an employer who has a business in the US. IT is our primary focus for now.
- F1 visa: A student visa, with STEM-MS programs being our fertile areas. You can avail loans for this. After 6 months, most universities allow you to do CPT (curriculum practical training), and after completing the course, you can do OPT (optional practical training) for up to 3 years. You can also apply for an H1 visa in the interim, and things will go smoothly with dedication and advice.
Loans: Government loans like Ambedkar Vidhyanidhi and Safari Karamchari are available, free up to 20 lakhs. Needy people will receive as much support as possible. Private loans from banks and third-party loans are also supported.
